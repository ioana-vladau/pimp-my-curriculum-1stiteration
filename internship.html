Internships take place in the 4th semester and must correspond to 15 ECTS.

The internship is concluded with an exam (internship exam). The exam is internal and assessed according to the 7-point grading scale. The internship is unpaid. During the internship, students are assigned a supervisor from the programme and a contact/supervisor from the host organisation.
